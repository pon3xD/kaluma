Play Mario Kart: Super Circuit

WARNING: The emulator saves the progress to the cache, so it's advised to use the Save State option whenever you want to save your game. If the cache is cleaned all progress will be lost

Description:

Mario Kart: Super Circuit is the third game in the Mario Kart Spin-off series, and the first Mario Kart game to ever reach a portable console. Race your way through 40 different tracks with 8 iconic characters from the Super Mario universe in this fast paced kart racing game

Controls:

Steer with Left/right arrow keys

Z -> Accelerate

X -> Brake

A -> Item

S -> Hop/Drift(hold)

Enter -> Pause

Shift -> Honk

Down arrow+X -> Reverse

A+S -> Access extra tracks (if unlocked)
