Play Subway Surfers

Description:

Subway Surfers is an endless runner game. The objective is to control characters running along subway tracks, avoiding obstacles and oncoming trains. Players collect coins and power-ups while evading the pursuing inspector and his dog. The goal is to run as far as possible, achieving high scores and unlocking new content in this fast-paced and visually engaging game.

Controls:

Up arrow -> Jump

Down arrow -> Roll

Left/Right arroww -> Change rail

Spacebar -> Hoverboard
