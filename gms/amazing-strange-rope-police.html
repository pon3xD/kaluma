Play Amazing Strange Rope Police

Description:

Turn into Spider guy and complete missions in Vice Vegas. Swing around, practice some karate moves and have fun in this city sandbox game! As you get money, you can buy weapon, skins and hats to further enhance your experience.

Controls:

Move with WASD

Spacebar -> Jump

Shift -> Run

F -> Get into Vehicles

Q -> Shoot web

LMB/ctrl -> Attack

RMB -> Laser

Scroll -> Change Weapons
