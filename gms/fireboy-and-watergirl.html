Play Fireboy and Watergirl

Description:

Fireboy and Watergirl is a cooperative puzzle-platformer game designed for two players. The objective is to navigate through levels filled with obstacles, puzzles, and hazards. Fireboy is immune to fire-related obstacles, while Watergirl is immune to water-related obstacles. Coordination and teamwork are key to successfully completing each level.

Controls:

Move Fireboy with the arrow keys

Move Watergirl with WASD
