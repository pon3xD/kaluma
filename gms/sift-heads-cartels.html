Play Sift Heads Cartels

Description:

Sift Heads Cartel is an action-packed shooting game. The objective is to control a skilled assassin and complete various missions, taking down members of a dangerous cartel. Players navigate through gritty environments, eliminate targets, and survive intense shootouts to progress in the game's storyline.

Controls:

Interact/Aim with the mouse

In Combat:

W -> Get up

S -> Duck

A/D -> Change Position

LMB -> Shoot

Spacebar -> Reload
