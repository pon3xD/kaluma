Play Super Mario World

WARNING: The emulator saves the progress to the cache, so it's advised to use the Save State option whenever you want to save your game. If the cache is cleaned all progress will be lost

Description:

Super Mario World is a classic platformer. The objective is to guide Mario, the iconic plumber, through various colorful levels in the Mushroom Kingdom. Players must navigate obstacles, defeat enemies, and rescue Princess Peach from the villainous Bowser. Collect power-ups, such as the Super Mushroom and Fire Flower, to enhance Mario's abilities and explore hidden secrets in this beloved side-scrolling adventure.

Controls:

Move with the arrow keys

Enter -> Select

Z -> Jump

X/S -> Dash/Use powerup

A -> Spin jump
