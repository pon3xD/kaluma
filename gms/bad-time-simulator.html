Play Bad Time Simulator

Description:

Experience the hardest encounter of the Award-Winning Indie game Undertale in this simulator. Battle Sans and find out who's the most determined.

Controls:

Select/Move the heart with the arrow keys

Z -> Accept

X -> Cancel

ProTips:

- When your heart turns blue, it starts getting afected by gravity. You can then jump with the up key.

- To not get damaged by the blue bones, do not move.
