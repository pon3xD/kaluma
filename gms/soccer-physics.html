Play Soccer Physics

Description:

Soccer Physics is a comical sports game with a physics-based twist. The objective is to control quirky characters in a two-player soccer match. The challenge lies in the intentionally awkward controls, leading to hilarious and unpredictable soccer actions. Score goals by maneuvering the characters and use unconventional tactics to outscore your opponent.

Controls:

Up arrow -> Jump blue team

W -> Jump red team
