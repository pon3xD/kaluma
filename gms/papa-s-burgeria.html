Play Papa's Burgeria

Description:

Papa's Burgeria is a restaurant simulation game where players manage a burger joint. The objective is to take orders, grill burgers, customize them with various toppings, and serve them to customers. Success depends on efficiency, accuracy, and keeping customers satisfied to earn tips and improve the restaurant's reputation.

Controls:

Select the menu items with the mouse
