Play Bob the Robber

Description:

Bob the Robber is a popular online stealth and strategy game where players control Bob, a skilled thief. The objective is to guide Bob through various levels, avoiding security measures and guards, to successfully steal valuable items and complete heists. Players need to use their wit, timing, and stealth to overcome obstacles and reach their goal without getting caught.

Controls:

Move with left/right

Climb/acccess terminals/read notes with the up arrow

Go down stairs with the down arrow
