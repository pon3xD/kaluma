Play Stickman Hook

Description:

Stickman Hook is a physics-based platformer. The objective is to control a stickman swinging through various levels using a grappling hook. Players must navigate the stickman through obstacles, time swings accurately, and reach the end of each level. The game emphasizes momentum and precision as players swing, jump, and avoid obstacles to complete challenges.

Controls:

Hold to Swing
